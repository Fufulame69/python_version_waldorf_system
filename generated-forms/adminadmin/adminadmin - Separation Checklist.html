Employee Separation Checklist

Management must process terminations promptly and ensure all terminated team members are terminated in PeopleSoft and their access removed or disabled from all other systems immediately (IE: within 14 days from the date of termination). Involuntary terminations should be processed immediately.

Employee Information: The form should be completed in its entirety and signed by the supervisor for processing.

Separation Information: List the date access should be removed and date processed.

Network & Applications Access: Mark the appropriate systems / access to be terminated. Note: Management must process terminations and ensure all current network and computer applications access and accounts are terminated or removed immediately upon termination (IE: within 14 days). This includes PeopleSoft, OnQ Operations Audit, OnQ PMS, etc.
